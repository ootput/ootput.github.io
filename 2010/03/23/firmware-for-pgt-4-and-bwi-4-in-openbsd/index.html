Firmware for pgt(4) and bwi(4) in OpenBSD

OpenBSD does not provide closed firmware in their installation sets; You must manually install the required firmware packages. As root:

# pkg_add -ivvv http://www.nazgul.ch/bwi/bwi-firmware-1.4.tgz

# pkg_add -ivvv http://www.nazgul.ch/pgt/pgt-firmware-1.2.tgz
